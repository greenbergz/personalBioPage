Studies

I am currentlly a sophomore at Washington University in St.Louis studying computer science and economics

I have been working with programming since I was a sophomore in high school. My senior year of high school I started working in robotics by working with VEX robots. I had to get my specific robot to use its claw to lift up a cup. It used sensors to follow a path mapped out by black tape. Eventually we moved on to quad-copters and got them to fly.

Since coming to college I have worked on several projects such as a bouncing balls physics simulator, and a picture of a flower using recursive design. In my sophomore year I started working with an Arduino board to work with two processors at once. I have used various sensors on the board to get it to act like a fit bit, a morse code translator, and a temperature sensor.

In my studies of economics I have written papers about why NASA is loosing funding and why they are not able to continue their work a strongly as they once were able to.
